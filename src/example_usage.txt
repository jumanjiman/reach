
The `reach` command is a simple wrapper around `ping`.

Benefits:

* Check exit status to determine reachability of a host or IP
* No output to /dev/stdout
* Errors go to /dev/stderr

Trivial example:

for x in $(seq 1 100); do
  hostname=server${x}.example.com
  echo ========= $hostname ===========
  reach $hostname || continue
  ssh $hostname "uptime"
done
